Your Neighbourhood Artisian Coffee Shop

Artisian Coffee + Tea in the Heart Of LOS FELIZ.

At Brū, it all starts with love. From the careful sourcing of quality ingredients, to a deep investment in the lives of the coffee farmers who cultivate the beans, we strive to honor coffee farmers from around the world. With meticulous care we prepare handcrafted coffee that starts your day. We like to think that our coffee is rich with inspiration—and hope that you can taste it in every sip.


We invite you to to join us and experience our ever-changing seasonal sweets, savories, chocolates and caramels. We also offer a wide selection of house made sodas, syrups made from scratch and hand blended teas. So, whether you're grabbing something to go or nestling in for a while, consider Brū your neighborhood haven, where coffee and community are infused with love. Come visit us today.
